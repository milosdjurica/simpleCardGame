Welcome to our simple card game!

Please choose how you want to count points :

1. Every card counts one point. Player with most cards is winner

2. Cards higher than 10 are worth 10 points. Others are worth their value (9 = 9)

RULES :

1. Who gets higher card, wins round. Winner of round takes both cards.

2. If it is tie, then next round decide and winner of that round also takes cards from tie round.

3. Winner of the game is player who has most cards

3. Winner of the game is player who has most points. Numbers from 2 to 9 are worth 2-9 points. Numbers higher than 10 are worth 10 points
